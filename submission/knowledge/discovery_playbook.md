# Discovery Playbook: Validate Before You Build

Source: QuestForge Bootstrap Library (original, MIT-licensed)

## Customer pain mapping
- Run 25 problem interviews before writing a line of product code.
- Ask about the last time they hit the problem, not whether they "would" use a tool.
- Look for willingness to pay: 10+ of 25 should name a budget or current workaround cost.

## Sharpening the ICP
- Narrow until the customer is a single sentence: role, company size, trigger event.
- A 10-person SaaS targeting small teams should pick one wedge segment, not "all SMBs".
- Reject vanity segments that cannot be reached with $0 channels.

## Signals you are ready to move on
- One documented ICP with a named trigger.
- A repeatable pain story heard from at least 8 interviews.
- A wedge that one specialist can own end to end.
