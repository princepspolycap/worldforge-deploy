# GTM and Finance Playbook: First 100 Customers

Source: QuestForge Bootstrap Library (original, MIT-licensed)

## $0 acquisition channels
- Content / SEO: lowest CAC over time, slowest to ramp. Budget 8 weekly hours.
- Community (Reddit, niche Slacks): fast feedback, CAC under $10 when authentic.
- Founder-led outbound: highest CAC but best learning; cap at 5 weekly hours.

## Financial guardrails for a bootstrapped SaaS
- Target MRR ramp should be monotonic month over month for the first 6 months.
- Keep burn under your 6-month MRR target so breakeven lands by month 6.
- Track churn from day one; a healthy bootstrapped SaaS holds monthly churn under 5%.

## Launch sequence
- Warm list email on day 0, community posts day 1-3, partnerships week 2.
- Measure activation, not signups: the first five minutes must deliver one clear win.
